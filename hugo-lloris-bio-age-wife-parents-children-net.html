Hugo Lloris Bio, Age, Wife, Parents, Children, Net Worth

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 19, 2024

Hugo Lloris is a very hardworking goalkeeper who rose through the ranks of the France National Team.

He has worked hard to be where he is which seems to impress a lot of fans.

This article talks about Hugo Lloris’s biography, age, height, wife, parents, children, net worth and everything fans need to know about him.

Hugo Lloris Biography

Hugo Hadrien Dominique Lloris is a professional French footballer who plays in the English Premier League.

Lloris started his career when he joined Nice B in 2004-2005. He had 2 years there before getting promoted to the Nice main team. He was with the Nice team for 3 years which sums up to 78 appearances for both sides.

He departed Nice but remained in the French League 1 joining Lyon in 2008-2009. Hugo Lloris managed to pull 201 appearances for Lyon in his 5 amazing seasons.

Hugo Lloris switched leagues in 2012-2013 when he joined Tottenham Hotspur. He is currently the captain of Tottenham Hotspur and has made a total of 437 appearances in his 11 seasons for the club.

How old is Hugo Lloris?

Hugo Lloris was born in Nice which is in France. He was born on 26th December 1986 and is currently 35 years.

What is Hugo Lloris Height?

The French goalkeeper is 6ft 2in. He makes extraordinary saves and mostly depends on his height to make these saves.

What position does Hugo Lloris play?

Hugo Lloris is one of the best goalkeepers in this generation of football.

Hugo Lloris Career

Hugo Hadrien Dominique started his career when he was 18 years. He has since then made a total of 736 appearances so far. He has won the Coupe de France and Trophée des Champions.

He had his first International call up in 2008 and has been with the France National Team for over a decade. He was part of the French Squad that won the FIFA World Cup in 2018 and the UEFA Nations League in 2020-2021 and is currently the captain of the French squad.

Who is Hugo Lloris Wife?

Hugo Lloris got married to his long-time girlfriend, Marine Lloris in 2012. Though they started their relationship at a very young age that did not stop them from coming this far and even making it to marriage now.

The couple has kept everything else about their relationship private and away from the cameras.

Hugo Lloris Parents

Luc Lloris is the father of the French goalkeeper, Hugo Lloris. He is an International Law Specialist which was enough for him to be his son’s agent.

His mother, Marie Lloris, however, passed away in April 2008 after battling breast cancer for a while.

Hugo Lloris Children

Hugo and Marine Lloris have been blessed with three amazing children. Anna Rose, Leandro, and Giuliano Lloris are the children of a loving couple.

The parents have always provided whatever the kids will need and have also given them all the love children could ever wish for.
