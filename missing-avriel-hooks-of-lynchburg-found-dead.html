Missing Avriel Hooks of Lynchburg Found Dead

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 30, 2024

Lynchburg, VA – The disappearance of 20-year-old Avriel Hooks has devastated the Lynchburg, Virginia community after the missing woman was found deceased just one week after vanishing. Hooks’ tragic death has left more questions than answers as police investigate suspicious circumstances surrounding her disappearance.

Hooks, described as a responsible young woman not prone to unexpected absences, was last seen by her mother on August 22nd when she was dropped off from work at home. The next day, Hooks failed to show up to her job at the Lynchburg Public Library, prompting worried family members to report her missing.

The Lynchburg Police Department quickly labeled the disappearance as suspicious and mobilized search efforts across the city and surrounding counties. Friends and family also aided in the extensive search, hoping to find Hooks safe.

After nearly a week pursuing leads without success, investigators caught a tragic break in the case on August 28th. A body was discovered in a wooded area off U.S. Route 60 in Amherst County, approximately 20 miles from Lynchburg.

While authorities have not yet confirmed the identity, sources indicate it is likely Hooks. The medical examiner will make the final determination pending an autopsy.

Shortly after locating the body, Lynchburg police brought in a person of interest for questioning. The individual, now identified as 21-year-old Michael Perry III of Lynchburg, has been arrested and charged with second-degree murder and the unlawful disposal of human remains.

Police have not disclosed details on how Perry may have known Hooks or revealed a suspected motive. However, the charges imply detectives believe Perry is responsible for Hooks’ death and dumping her body.

For now, authorities are releasing limited information as they continue gathering evidence and building a timeline of Hooks’ disappearance and Perry’s movements. Her family and the Lynchburg community are desperate for answers and justice.

The tragic outcome has cast a dark cloud over Lynchburg, where many residents joined search teams and held out hope that Hooks would be found alive. Instead, they are now mourning the loss of a promising young woman who lit up every room she entered, according to loved ones.

Avriel Hooks’ bright smile and bubbly personality will be deeply missed. Friends described her as a gifted artist and talented singer who dreamed of starting her own inclusive nonprofit.

While her light was senselessly dimmed far too soon, Hooks’ spirit lives on in the hearts of all who knew her. As Lynchburg struggles to process this tragedy, many have come together to honor her memory and seek justice.

The police investigation remains active and ongoing. Anyone with information related to Hooks’ disappearance and death is urged to contact the Lynchburg Police Department. Tips can also be submitted anonymously through Central Virginia Crime Stoppers.

For now, the Lynchburg community is focused on supporting Hooks’ grieving family as they navigate profound loss. Though the tragic outcome was not what many hoped for, the outpouring of love and tireless efforts to find Hooks highlight the best of this city – and her memory will never be forgotten.
