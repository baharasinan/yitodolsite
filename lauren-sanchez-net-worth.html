Lauren Sanchez Finance Overview

Career Beginnings

Lauren’s career began humbly as a desk assistant at KCOP-TV. Her dedication and talent quickly saw her rise through the ranks. She later worked as an anchor and reporter at KTVK-TV in Phoenix, gaining valuable experience that paved the way for bigger opportunities.

Major Career Milestones

Lauren’s major breakthrough came with her role on the entertainment show Extra. She became a familiar face on television, hosting Good Day LA and working as a reporter for Fox Sports Net. Her versatility and charisma earned her an Emmy nomination, highlighting her impact in the industry.

Business Ventures and Black Ops Aviation

In 2016, Lauren took a bold step by founding Black Ops Aviation, the first female-owned aerial film and production company.
