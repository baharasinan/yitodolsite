Kim Kardashian responds to Chloe Moretz, Bette Midler and Piers Morgan's negative comments on Twitte

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, September 7, 2024

    Kim Kardashian has called out a string of celebrities who poked fun at her latest nude selfie. The social media queen shared a half-censored throwback photo of herself on Monday, and captioned it: "When you're like I have nothing to wear LOL."

    Kim received some negative comments from the showbiz world, with Piers Morgan leading the tweets. The British presenter made a reference to Kim's husband Kanye West, who last week openly admitted that he is £37million in debt.

    "I know the old man's $50 million in debt, Kim – but this is absurd. Want me to buy you some clothes?" quipped Piers.

    Kim Kardashian told Piers Morgan to "never offer to buy a married woman clothes"

    Kim then responded, writing "never offer to buy a married woman clothes" and "sorry I'm late to the party guys I was busy cashing my 80 million video game check & transferring 53 million into our joint account."

    The star tweeted back: "Fairly sure @kanyewest just seized control of @KimKardashian's phone."

    Piers wasn't the only celebrity who fell foul of the star. Bette Midler and Chloe Moretz joined forces, with Chloe retweeting Bette's post that read: "Kim Kardashian tweeted a nude selfie today. If Kim wants us to see a part of her we've never seen, she's gonna have to swallow the camera."

    "Hey @BetteMidler I know it's past your bedtime but if you're still up and reading this send nudes #justkidding," Kim responded to the 70-year-old actress.

    She added: "Hey @BetteMidler I really didn't want to bring up how you sent me a gift awhile back trying to be a fake friend then come at me #dejavu."

    To 19-year-old Chloe, Kim wrote: "Let's all welcome @ChloeGMoretz to Twitter, since no one knows who she is. Your nylon cover is cute boo."

    While Bette has not responded, rising star Chloe politely gave her opinion on "respecting the platform that you're given as a celebrity".

    Following Kim's fiery tweets, the mum-of-two shared another nude selfie, which she captioned "#liberated".
