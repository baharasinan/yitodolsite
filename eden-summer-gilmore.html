Eden Summer Gilmore portrayed young Betty Cooper in Riverdale! Know her Age, Birthday, Net Worth!

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Since its premiere, Riverdale has gone on to become one of the most popular series worldwide. Eden Summer Gilmore, a Canadian actress, earned wide recognition after appearing as the younger version of Betty Cooper in Riverdale.

The 15-year-old actress has made appearances in other shows such as Nancy Drew, Supernatural, The Good Doctor, etc. Besides, she has worked in a few movies as well.

A Canadian citizen, Eden was born on August 16, 2005, in Canada. However, several online tabloids have stated her nationality as American. Now, let's know some interesting facts about Gilmore.

Eden Summer Gilmore Relationship Status

The adorable Eden is still young to be involved in a romantic relationship. Hence, she doesn't have a boyfriend at the moment. However, Millie Bobby Brown and Jacob Sartorius began dating each other in their early teens. Talking about Eden, she is primarily focused on her career.

Besides acting, Gilmore loves singing, and she plays the ukulele as well. Still a high school student, Eden hasn't revealed if she will date any guy soon. She once revealed that her main focus is to star in a big-budgeted movie and work with established actors.

Net Worth

Eden Summer Gilmore's net worth is unavailable. But she earns a desirable income from her acting career. At just 15, Gilmore has worked in many hit shows. The television actors are known to have earned up to six-figure digits per episode. On average, actors might earn somewhere between $32,455-$78,654.

Gilmore began her career as a child model. Later, she bagged roles in television and films. Her breakthrough role came as young Betty Cooper in CW's hit show, Riverdale. The series has ensemble cast members, including Charles Melton, KJ Apa, Lili Reinhart, Camila Mendes, etc.

The rising star, Miss Eden, has studied acting from institutes such as Rogue Studios, Mcllroy & Associates, etc. A Vancouver based agency called Red Talent Management represents Gilmore.

Movies and TV Shows

As mentioned, Eden started acting when she was five years old, mainly in commercials. Her first major role was in Riverdale. Then she appeared in an episode of Supernatural titled Wayward Sisters alongside Kathryn Newton.

In 2018, Gilmore bagged Abby's role in the Hallmark channel movie, It's Christmas, Eve. She portrayed the younger version of Nancy in the series Nancy Drew in 2019. Likewise, Eden appeared as Tia in The Healing Powers of Dude.

In 2021, Eden appeared in two tv series, The Good Doctor and Family Law. Moreover, some of her short movie credits include 20 Minutes to Life (2018), MoonTime (2019), Sabby & Szabo (2019), and Where Everyone Lives (2020).

Eden Summer Gilmore Awards

Just in a few years, Eden has stunned audiences with her amazing performances. The talented actress has earned nominations in several awards and has won some. She has two Joey Awards; one for starring in Supernatural (2018) and one for It's Christmas, Eve (2019).

Furthermore, Eden won the Young Entertainer Awards for starring in the short movie 20 Minutes to Life (2019). The same year she won the Young Artist Awards.
