Jimmy O Yang and Brianne Kimmel get new Dalmatian puppy named Lucy

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Chef/comedian Jimmy O Yang and venture capitalist Brianne Kimmel have revealed their first fur baby together: Lucy the Dalmatian puppy! The couple shared a joint Instagram post, where they mention the pup’s model good looks, one blue eye, and preference for her mama. Like her humans, Lucy is also a big foodie.

A few celebrity friends commented on the post. The first being Lucy Hale’s approval of the name. Nina Dobrev commented, “Omg stop. I love her nose freckles!” Yang’s Crazy Rich Asians costars Nico Santos and Gemma Chan left a few heart-eyed and teary-eyed face emojis.

Jimmy O Yang and his family have an Insta-famous pug named Toffee Star with 15k followers – We’ll just have to wait to see when (not if) Lucy gets her own Instagram account. As a fan and a dog dad, this writer also hopes he’ll resurrect his “Jimmy’s Kitchen” YouTube series with a few Asian-inspired dishes for dogs.
