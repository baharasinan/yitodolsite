Acting career

Nia has appeared in several theatre performances.

In 2019, Nia appeared in Neil Gaiman’s The Ocean at the End of the Lane play at the National Theatre and the Duke of York Theatre.

She’s also performed in There is a War, Men & Women, Beast, and Divided Britain.

Nia’s professional acting debut was in 2019 when she appeared as an extra in the film Rocketman.

Before this role, also in 2019, she played Sophia in a short film titled They Found Her in a Field.

In 2020, Towle appeared in The Hollow, a made-for-television film, and a short film named Sparklers.

In 2021, she graduated from the Guildhall School of Music & Drama.

Nia also played the role of Maya in a short film titled ‘Late’ in 2021.

Nia’s biggest role to date came in 2022 when she played the role of Louisa Musgrove in the film Persuasion.

In December 2022, Towle was added to the cast list of The Lord of the Rings.
