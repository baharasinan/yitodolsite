Willow Smith is an American singer, rapper, actor, dancer, voiceover artist, and songwriter. The daughter of Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith, Willow has released various albums, extended plays, and tracks like Ardipithecus, Interdimensional Tesseract, The 1st, Willow, Whip My Hair, Transparent Soul (ft. Travis Barker), Why Don’t You Cry, 21st Century Girl, and Fireball (ft. Nicki Minaj). As she was signed by the record label Roc Nation, she became the youngest artist signed to the label. Willow Smith has also worked on shows and movies including Merry Madagascar, I Am Legend, Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa, Adventure Time, Kit Kittredge: An American Girl, and True Jackson, VP. Moreover, she started co-hosting the Facebook Watch talk show Red Table Talk in 2018 and it has earned her two NAACP Image Awards and two Daytime Emmy Award nominations.
