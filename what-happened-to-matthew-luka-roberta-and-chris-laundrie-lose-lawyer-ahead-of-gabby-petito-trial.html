Gabby Petito's parents' lawsuit against Chris and Roberta Laundrie

During an unfortunate four-month cross-country road trip in 2021, Brian fatally killed Petito in Utah. Brian returned to his parents' house in Florida after the murder on August 28, 2021.

Later, Petito's parents filed a lawsuit against Chris and Roberta for allegedly causing them severe anguish and the case is scheduled to go to trial in May 2024, per .

According to the complaint acquired by local CBS affiliate , "(They) knew that they could prevent such additional mental suffering and anguish... by disclosing what they knew about the well-being and location of the remains of Gabrielle Petito, yet they repeatedly refused to do so."

They are requesting more than $30,000 in compensation.
