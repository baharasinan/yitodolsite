Brittanya OCampo Wiki, Before Surgery, Net Worth, Children, Family

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Who is Brittanya O’Campo?

Brittanya O’Campo was born on 2 July 1985, in Oxnard, California, USA, and is a reality television and social media personality, best known for appearing in shows such as “Charm School”, and “Rock of Love Bus”. She’s also appeared in various magazines as a model throughout her career.

The Net Worth of Brittanya O’Campo

How rich is Brittanya O’Campo? As of early-2019, sources estimate a net worth that is at $8 million, earned largely through success in her various endeavors. She’s appeared on television, and handles a business, being the co-owner of the company 187, Inc. As she continues her career, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

Early Life

Brittanya grew up in a relatively big family, having four brothers and a sister, all of whom were very close and competitive, which helped develop her confidence at a young age. According to her, her siblings would become instrumental in her future development. However, there were also problems in their family when she was growing up, particularly when her father was sent to jail when she was 15 years old.

Before her father was sent to prison, he emphasized that the family should take care of one another. Despite what happened, the family did grow more supportive of one another and she was also aware that her father had a lifestyle that sent him to prison. However, details about why he was arrested haven’t been shared by her publicly. Her education is also not known, such as details of where she completed high school or if she went to college.

Reality Television – Rock of Love Bus

O’Campo’s popularity was boosted significantly when she was cast in the third season of “Rock of Love with Bret Michaels” on VH1 during its third season, entitled “Rock of Love Bus with Bret Michaels”. The season features numerous women travelling on a tour bus with Bret Michaels, as they battle for his attention and affection. During this season, the show made the announcement that it would be the final season for “Rock of Love”. She made it to fourth place in the competition, reaching the 10th episode before finally being eliminated.

The show was known to have a few production problem, including that while filming the driver of the truck hauling equipment for “Rock of Love Bus” feel asleep at the wheel and crossed into oncoming traffic on Interstate 57 in Illinois, hitting a sports utility vehicle (SUV) and a pickup truck, but no one was seriously injured. The driver in question’s license was suspended, and he was charged with a few other violations. VH1 later released a statement that no employees or crew were hurt. Production was temporarily halted for a short time at Michaels’ request.

Charm School

“Charm School” is another reality television series Brittanya became involved in, shortly after her appearance in “Rock of Love”. The show is a spin-off from “Flavor of Love”. The 13 contestants of each season compete in various challenges until one remains to win a cash prize of $100,000. She appeared during the third season of the show entitled “Charm School with Ricki Lake”, which contained many of the contestants from “Rock of Love Bus”, joined by contestants from “Real Chance of Love”. The show is known for acting as a finishing school, and helps the contestants develop proper etiquette. The winner of each season would gain the title of Charm School Queen.

During the third season, the contestants sought self-improvement through charity work and other selfless acts. Rick Lake is joined by deans played by radio personality Stryker and television producer Alani “La La” Vazquez. Brittanya managed to reach the top four once more, being eliminated during the ninth episode of the show. The season was eventually won by Ebony “Risky” Jones from “Real Chance of Love”, while former “Rock of Love Bus” contestants Marcia Alves and Ashley Klarich were second and third respectively. The third season was the final season of “Charm School”.

Personal Life and Current Endeavors

For her personal life, it is known that O’Campo married Lucky Moe Razavi, and they have five children together. Not many details have been shared about their family. After her appearances on television, her popularity on social media skyrocketed, resulting in her gaining numerous followers on her accounts on Facebook and Instagram, the latter having over 12 million followers. A lot of her online popularity comes from posts bordering on too revealing. She also admits that she has had some work done on her body to enhance some of her physical aspects.

Her popularity has allowed her to gain other opportunities as well. She quickly took the opportunity to become a model, and has appeared in over 20 magazines, including “Urban Ink”, ‘Inked” and “Savage”. She was also on the cover of “MMA Sports Magazine”, “Spire Magazine” and “Tattoo Energy”. She founded her own company entitled Avenue 187 which was later incorporated as 187, Inc. She started off by selling women’s clothing, but has since expanded to include clothing lines for men and children. She has also begun selling accessories, and most of her products can be shipped worldwide.
