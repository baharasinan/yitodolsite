Cody Garbrandt is a Mixed Martial Artist who has been a UFC Bantamweight Champion and Pinnacle Fighting Champion. He is also known as the 2nd person to defeat Dominick Cruz at UFC 207 in December 2016. Cody is also the co-author of the book titled The Pact. He has endorsed brands like Bud Light, Geico, Harley Davidson, Body Armor, and ONNIT Pro.

    Personal Trainer

    Cody Garbrandt trains 2-3 times for about 4-6 hours daily and takes a break only if he feels his body requires it. He trains hard during the first 3 days of the week and uses the 4th day to recover and work on maintenance and mobility, repeating this cycle throughout.

    As for his diet, Cody maintains a clean and healthy diet. Apart from breakfast, lunch, and dinner, he also snacks on either a smoothie or a bar in between the training to maintain his energy level until his next meal. He drinks gallons of water to remain hydrated. His training food chart includes –

    • Breakfast – He starts his day with eggs, turkey bacon, some toast, and oatmeal (occasionally) along with coffee mixed with MCT oil.
    • Lunch – His lunch consists of salmon, tilapia, or chicken, which is about 6 to 8 ounces of protein. He takes that with some rice, which has carbohydrates, along with vegetables like zucchini, broccoli, and asparagus.
    • Evening Snack – He then has a smoothie prepared with protein, berries, bananas, coconut water, and spinach for more greens with some almond butter.
    • Dinner – His dinner is similar to his lunch, composing of 6 to 8 ounces of protein. He takes ground turkey, few carbohydrates, and more of broccoli and greens.

    He also has a ritual meal (dinner) for the night before a fight which is comprised of salmon, spinach, sweet potatoes, some sushi, and rice.

    Cody Garbrandt Facts

  • His mother enrolled him and his brother in wrestling classes at a very young age.
  • At the age of 15, he started fighting, basically to stay out of the street fights and to stay focused.
  • He considers his mother, Jessica Enos, and Maddux Maple, his young friend battling leukemia who makes entrances with Cody to the ring, as his heroes.
  • Ever since he wore his first boxing glove, he had dreamed of fighting in the UFC.
  • His earlier jobs included working in the construction business and later as an MMA instructor.
  • Cody’s first trainer was his uncle, who was an amateur boxer.
  • His pre-fight prep includes reminding himself that he has people who love him and support him whether he wins or loses.
  • At the age of 14, his uncle gave him the nickname “No Love” because of his serious business-like attitude in the ring for Cody didn’t glove touch or shook hands and remained that way until the match ended.
  • In his free time, he likes checking out the places to eat in Sacramento, go for movies, and hang out with his teammates.
  • On May 8, 2018, his book titled The Pact, co-written with Mark Dagostino, was published by Harper Collins.
