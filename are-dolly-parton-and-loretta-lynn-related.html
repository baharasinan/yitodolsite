Are Dolly Parton and Loretta Lynn related?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 26, 2024

Dolly Rebecca Parton, an American singer-songwriter, actor, philanthropist, and businesswoman, was born on January 19, 1946. She is well known for her decades-long career in country music.

Avie Lee Caroline and Robert Lee Parton Sr. raised her as the fourth of twelve children. Rebecca (Dunn) Whitted, Parton’s maternal great-great-grandmother, inspired her middle name.

Parton’s father, known as “Lee,” labored in the East Tennessee mountains, initially as a sharecropper and eventually as the owner of his own modest tobacco farm and land. He also worked in construction to bolster the farm’s meager earnings.

Despite her father’s illiteracy, Parton has frequently stated that he was one of the most business-savvy persons she had ever met.

Are Dolly Parton and Loretta Lynn related?

Yes, they are related. They are sisters according to our sources. Jake Owens, the father of Avie Lee, was a Pentecostal minister, and Parton and her siblings were all devoted churchgoers.

Parton has long given credit to her mother’s family for her musical talent as well as her father for her business acumen.

As a small child, Parton’s family relocated from the Pittman Center neighborhood to a farm up on the neighboring Locust Ridge.

There, she spent the majority of her most treasured youth moments. Theme park Dollywood, which bears Parton’s name, now has a recreation of the Locust Ridge cabin.
