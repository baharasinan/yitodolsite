The 14 Amazon sliders Love Island fans are going crazy for

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 22, 2024

The Casa Amor folks love these sliders – which are selling out everywhere

    If you’re as obsessed with Love Island as we are, you must have noticed the super comfy-looking pillow sliders worn by the beautiful Casa Amor residents.

    RELATED: 8 Love Island style villas to rent this summer that are totally our type on paper

    This is a trend that first popped up last year, and in 2022 even the boys are rocking them - yes Ikenna Ekwonna and Davide Sanclimenti we're talking to you! In fact, Davide loves them so much he has them in two different colours!

    The shoes - which have been dubbed 'Love Island Sliders' by fans - are actually dupes of Kanye West’s Yeezy sliders, which are a collab with Adidas.

    Ultra-soft pillow slides, £14.99, Amazon

    SHOP NOW

    The Amazon version is an absolute steal at only £14.99 – and available in a range of neutral and autumnal shades, including olive green, white, beige and yellow.

    The original Yeezy slides went on sale for $55 but quickly sold out, with celeb fans including Kendall Jenner, Kim Kardashian and Molly-Mae Hague rocking them. You can still pick up an authentic pair for anywhere between £198 and £820 on Farfetch here if you do want the real thing.

    And the chic black brand new 2022 summer version is due to launch on July 25 - you can sign up for a chance to buy them via Adidas' official Yeezy page - but competition is likely to be fierce to grab a pair.
