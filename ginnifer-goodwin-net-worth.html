FAQs about Ginnifer Goodwin

Who is Ginnifer Goodwin?

Ginnifer Goodwin is an American actress best known for her roles in Once Upon a Time as Snow White/Mary Margaret Blanchard, Zootopia as the voice of Judy Hopps, and Big Love as Margene Heffman.

What is her real name?

Her real name is Jennifer Michelle Goodwin. She changed the spelling to Ginnifer to distinguish her name and emphasize its Southern pronunciation.

When was she born?

She was born on May 22, 1978, in Memphis, Tennessee.

Is Ginnifer Goodwin married?

Yes, she is married to actor Josh Dallas, her co-star from Once Upon a Time. They got married on April 12, 2014.

Does she have children?

Ginnifer and Josh Dallas have two sons: Oliver Finlay Dallas, born in May 2014, and Hugo Wilson Dallas, born in June 2016.

What are some notable films and TV shows Ginnifer Goodwin has appeared in?

Besides Once Upon a Time and Zootopia, she starred in Big Love, He’s Just Not That Into You, Mona Lisa Smile, Something Borrowed, and the series Why Women Kill.

What awards has she won?

How did she start her acting career?

Goodwin graduated from Boston University’s School of Fine Arts and began her career with roles in television series such as Law & Order and Ed.
