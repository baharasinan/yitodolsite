JT Says Lil Uzi Vert Is Her Favorite Thing To Eat Post BET Spat

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 29, 2024

JT has hilariously claimed that her choice of edible consumption is Lil Uzi Vert rather than a specific food or snack as the pair were riding around together.

On Saturday (July 1), The Neighborhood Talk reposted a video on Instagram of the other half of the City Girls duo chilling inside of Uzi’s brand new custom made Rolls-Royce Cullinan. With no car roof in sight, JT was asked by someone what’s her favorite food to eat. With a bright smile, she playfully pointed at Lil Uzi Vert while they were seated next to her and replied, “him.”

AD LOADING...

This is the second time the couple were seen out together after their phone-throwing scuffle at the 2023 BET Awards.The first time was during Lil Uzi Vert’s listening party for his album Pink Tape at New York City’s Irving Plaza on Tuesday (June 27).

In a video from the event, the Philadelphia and Miami rappers were seen dancing together as a song from the project blared through the speakers, with Uzi holding JT’s hand and spinning her around.

AD LOADING...

More footage from the party showed Uzi casually eating a Filet-O-Fish from McDonald’s as they pulled up to the venue. Lil Baby and Trippie Redd were also in attendance to support their friend and collaborator.

Lil Uzi Vert and JT’s relationship drama was on full display at the BET Awards on Sunday (June 25) as the City Girls star was filmed angrily throwing a phone at her boyfriend and calling them “a bitch-ass n-gga” and “a groupie” while sat in the audience.

Rumors suggested JT was upset that Uzi posed for a photo with Ice Spice, who they name-dropped while performing during the awards show. “I’m fucking the dogshit out of this bitch, her wig came off twice/ She had a lil’ fro, she look like Ice Spice,” they rapped on the song.

However, a rep for JT told TMZ her blow-up “had nothing to do with Ice” and instead she “had become frustrated when she lost her seat after the audience shuffled around.”

Meanwhile, Lil Uzi Vert may be the artist to give Hip Hop its first No. 1 of 2023 thanks to the release of The Pink Tape. The 27-year-old rapper released the long-awaited project on Friday (June 30) and it’s already generating massive numbers. Given the pace at which it is moving, the project is well on its way to topping the Billboard 200 next week.

AD LOADING...

According to Chart Data, all 26 songs on The Pink Tape made it on the Spotify US’s Top 100. This marks Uzi’s biggest streaming day ever on the platform, with more than 60 million streams on June 30.

Additionally, RapTV has reported that the album is projected to move between 220–250,000 equivalent units in its first week.

AD LOADING...

One of the most popular songs from Pink Tape is “Endless Fashion” featuring Nicki Minaj. The knockout collaboration debuted at No. 6 on the US Spotify charts with 1.617 million streams.
