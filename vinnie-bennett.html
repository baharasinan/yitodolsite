Vinnie Bennett Career – Actor

After his a lot of hard work, he got a chance to appear in the ‘Fast & Furious 9’ film in 2021 and he played the role of Young Dom. He also worked in Kura, Black Sea Golden Ladder: The Visual Album, and other movies and series.
