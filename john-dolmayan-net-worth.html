What is John Dolmayan's Net Worth?

John Dolmayan is a Lebanese Armenian-American songwriter and drummer who has a net worth of $16 million. John Dolmayan was born in Beirut, Lebanon in July 1972. He is best known for being the drummer of the rock band System of a Down. Dolmayan is also the drummer for the band Indicator and formerly for the band Scars on Broadway. He joined System of a Down in 1997. The band released their self-titled debut studio album in 1998. Their second album Toxicity was released in 2001 and was certified 3x Platinum and reached #1 in the US and Canada.

System of a Down released the album Steal This Album! in 2002. Their album Mezmerize was released in 2005 and reached #1 in the US, Australia, Canada, Germany, New Zealand, and Switzerland. Their 2005 album Hypnotize also reached #1 in the US, Canada, Finland, and New Zealand. Their singles "Aerials" and "Hypnotize" both reached #12 on the US Alternative chart. System of a Down won a Grammy Award for Best Hard Rock Performance in 2006.
